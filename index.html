About Me

I am a UK Space Agency Aurora Research Fellow within the geophysics and petrology research groups in the School of Earth Sciences at the University of Bristol.


I am currently working on projects funded by the UK Space Agency (UK), NASA (USA), NSF (USA) and the NSFC (China). Details of these projects can be found below.

Projects

I have worked on many aspects of Earth and planetary evolution. Here are a few of my ongoing projects.

The Interior Structure and Evolution of Mars

I am currently one of the Science Team working on the InSight Mission, which will land on Mars in November this year. My work focuses on how we can use seismic snapshots of Mars' interior to tell us about Mars' chemical evolution.
